Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to my first meeting?

Bring all relevant legal documents, notices, identity proof, and a chronological timeline of events to help us assess your matter quickly.

How long will my case take to resolve?

Legal timelines vary significantly based on the judicial forum (Court vs. Tribunal) and case complexity. We provide an estimated roadmap after the initial review.

Can you represent me in any court in India?

While our primary presence is in Maharashtra (Kalyan, Mumbai, Thane), we handle appellate matters across major forums and tribunals nationwide.
